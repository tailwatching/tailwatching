If you signed up for "The Tail Watcher", here is what to expect:

Receive your license key via email
You should receive a license key in your email inbox. This license key serves as the password for your dashboard. The license key is designed to be long. Please feel free to drop me an email if you would like to change the key to a shorter one.

Receive a unique link to your dashboard via email
In 3 - 5 business days, I would create and deploy a dedicated instance of the dashboard for you. Once that is completed, I would drop you the unique link to your live dashboard.

Using the same institutional modelling tools used with B2B partners, I have developed trend following models for each asset in the dashboard. An average of 20 years of live data was used for each asset, creating live trend signals backed by decades of positive expectancy. The dashboard is a culmination thousands of research & modelling hours packed into a table of actionable insights for you.

The dashboard can serve the following purposes:
  • Tail watching: Spot fat tails that are often missed by retail investors, and capitalize on potential upside
  • Trend filtering: Identify the latest trending stocks across multiple exchanges in a snapshot
  • Market heatmap: Feel the pulse of the market across various asset classes, geographies and industries
  • Entry signals: Use the signals as standalone or complimentary data points to build your portfolio

The dashboard updates every time you visit the page. You can also manually refresh the table using the controls in the dashboard.

The stock exchanges included are: NYSE, NASDAQ, LON, HKSE.
The list is expected to grow as I continue to include more markets.

How does the Tail Watching model work?


Traditional portfolio strategies follow the idea that asset prices follow a normal distribution. However, the existence of fat tails suggests that asset returns are not normally distributed, but skewed.

The fat tails indicate that there is a probability (often larger than anticipated), that prices will move beyond 3 standard deviations. The Tail Watching model capitalizes on these tails in liquid markets to generated outsized returns.
